General

FARADY S11-M.RL, S13-M.RL series of three-dimensional triangular wound core transformer breaking the traditional planar structure, use symmetrical three-phase three-dimensional structure, the product is completely symmetrical three-phase core magnetic circuit reluctance greatly reduced, excitation current and significantly reduce the unload loss,which is a use of traditional materials,

but runs quieter, more compact energy-efficient transformers.

Its energy saving, environmental protection material on the outstanding performance of the province, and in full compliance with the energy policy.
